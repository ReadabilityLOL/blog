At this point, it’s a bit too much for me. I’m about to give up when I google duckduckgo what to do and find that I can use flatpak. I run sudo pacman -S flatpak and flatpak install flathub com.valvesoftware.Steam, and it actually works. My eyes tear up in joy, my heart pounds in happiness. I open steam and try to create an account. I type in my email, press on the captcha, and then it happens.
